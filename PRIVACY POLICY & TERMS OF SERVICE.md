### I. PRIVACY POLICY

#### 1. Data We Collect

Mercury Vault is designed with privacy in mind. We do **not** collect personal identifiers such as your full name, email address, or phone number.

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Collected |
|---|---|---|
| **RevenueCat** | Manage subscriptions & validate purchases | Device ID, purchase receipts |
| **Firebase Firestore** | Store leaderboard scores & seasonal events | Player alias, score, country code |
| **Firebase Crashlytics** | Monitor app performance & fix bugs | Anonymized crash reports |
| **Google Play Billing** | Process in-app purchases (Android) | Purchase tokens (no card data stored) |
| **Apple App Store** | Process in-app purchases (iOS) | Purchase tokens (no card data stored) |

#### 2. Leaderboard & Game Data

When you participate in Mercury Vault's global leaderboards powered by Firebase:

- Your **player alias** (chosen by you) and **score** are stored in Firebase Firestore and visible to other players worldwide.
- Your **country** (entered voluntarily or detected from device locale) may be shown for regional filtering.
- Leaderboard data is stored per game (Alchemist, Oracle Quiz, etc.) and filtered by time period (Today, This Week, All Time).
- You may request deletion of your leaderboard data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

#### 3. Advertising

**Mercury Vault contains zero advertisements.** We do not use AdMob or any advertising network. The app is completely ad-free for all users, including free-tier players.

#### 4. In-App Purchases

- All financial transactions are processed securely by **Apple App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play** (Android).
- We do **not** store credit card numbers or banking information.
- Purchase validation is handled by **RevenueCat** using encrypted receipt tokens only.
- Available plans: Monthly ($1.99), Yearly ($9.99), Lifetime ($59.99).

#### 5. Local Data Storage

- Your game progress, settings, and language preference are stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage.
- This data never leaves your device unless you participate in leaderboards.
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

#### 6. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:
- **Access** data we hold about you (leaderboard entries)
- **Delete** your leaderboard data upon request
- **Opt out** of leaderboards by not submitting scores

To exercise these rights, contact: **teams.hi@icloud.com**

#### 7. Children's Privacy

Mercury Vault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us immediately.

#### 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
