Part I
Privacy Policy
1 Introduction

Mercury Labs Studio ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your full name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily contact us for support.

2 Third-Party Services

Depending on the specific app you use, we may integrate one or more of the following third-party services:

Not all services are active in every app. Each app's store listing specifies which services are in use.

3 Advertising

Some of our apps include advertisements served by Google AdMob. Ads are only shown to free-tier users; premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

If an app contains no ads, this will be clearly stated in the app description. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (off)
4 Leaderboards & Cloud Data

For apps featuring global leaderboards or cloud-synced content powered by Firebase:

  • Your player alias (chosen by you) and score are stored in Firebase Firestore and may be visible to other users worldwide.
  • Your country (entered voluntarily or inferred from device locale) may be displayed for regional filtering.
  • Leaderboard data is scoped per app and per time period (Today, This Week, All Time).
  • You may request deletion of your leaderboard data at any time by contacting us at hi@mercurylabsstudio.com.
5 Local Data Storage
  • Your game progress, app settings, and language preferences are stored locally on your device using standard mobile storage mechanisms (e.g., AsyncStorage on React Native).
  • This data does not leave your device unless a specific feature (such as leaderboard submission) explicitly syncs it to the cloud.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.
6 In-App Purchases
  • All financial transactions are processed securely by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). We never handle your payment details directly.
  • We do not store credit card numbers, bank account information, or any financial credentials.
  • Purchase validation and subscription status are managed by RevenueCat using encrypted receipt tokens only.
  • Pricing varies by app and region. Refer to each app's store listing for current pricing.
7 Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data through one of our apps, please contact us immediately at hi@mercurylabsstudio.com and we will promptly delete the information.

8 User Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your leaderboard entries or cloud data
  • Opt-out — Decline leaderboard participation by not submitting scores
  • Portability — Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: hi@mercurylabsstudio.com. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

9 Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. Firebase data is secured with Firestore security rules. Purchase data is handled entirely by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat — all of which maintain industry-standard security certifications.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our apps evolve. We will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this document when changes are made. Continued use of our apps after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
